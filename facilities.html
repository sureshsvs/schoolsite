Facilities

Computer Lab

We have renovated , spacious computer lab with 20 systems. It has update version of computers and internet facility. It also has projector for teaching.

Chemistry Lab

A well equipped lab which can accommodate with 30 students at a time. Individual apparatus is given to each and every student. A distilled water plant has been installed.

Biology Lab

The bio lab is equipped with many compound and dissection microscopes. Spacious and can accommodate with 30 students at a time.

Physics Lab

The Physics lab is spacious and can accommodate 35 students at a time. It has a supporting with power backup with 3 phase connection. Equipments are of advanced version

Library

Library is a store house of knowledge. Keeping this in mind we have a well-lit, airy room which can accommodate 20 students at a time. It houses more than 2500 books belonging to different sections such as Literature, Mathematics, Economics, Tamil, Commerce, Religion, Social and Reference.

Smart Class

A smart classroom is a digital classroom which is an advanced form of a school that follows different ways of teaching to improve efficiency . We have 15 numbers of well equipped smart class rooms in our school.

Cctv

Saraswathi Vidyalaya is under electronic surveillance at all times

Scholarship

Government scholarship are arranged for minority students for std I to XII.

Transport Facility

Transports

School transport facility is available from our school within a radius of about 20 km in all directions.

In our school we have 9 numbers of vehicles.

All the vehicles in our school are properly maintained as per government rules and all the drivers and cleaners in our school have more than 10 years of experience to ensure the safety of our school students.

The school management is providing school transport facility to the students and staff members.

The transport process is closely monitored and supervised to ensure the safety of every child
